Ranver Singh

Dhurandhar is a massive Indian spy action thriller franchise directed by Aditya Dhar. Ranveer Singh stars as Hamza Ali Mazari (born as Jaskirat Singh Rangi), an Indian operative who infiltrates the Karachi underworld to track down the architects of major terror attacks against India.

Akshaye Khanna

Akshaye Vinod Khanna is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Hindi films. Known for his acting versatility and strong portrayals, he has appeared in over 40 films. Khanna is often regarded as one of the finest actors in Hindi cinema. He is a recipient of numerous accolades including two Filmfare Awards

Rakesh Bedi

Rakesh Bedi is an Indian actor who works in Hindi cinema. He has appeared in more than 150 of films since the 1970s, with many television and stage shows. He is known for his iconic comic roles in films such as Chashme Buddoor and Mera Damad

Arjun Rampal

Arjun Rampal stars as the ruthless antagonist, Major Iqbal, in the blockbuster spy-thriller duology Dhurandhar (2025) and Dhurandhar: The Revenge (2026), directed by Aditya Dhar. His chilling, measured portrayal of a Pakistani intelligence mastermind earned him some of the best reviews of his career

Danish Pandor

Danish Pandor is an Indian actor and model who works in Hindi films and television. He is best known for playing Pakistani gangster Uzair Baloch in the spy action thriller duology Dhurandhar and Dhurandhar: The Revenge.
